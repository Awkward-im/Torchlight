class description:
------------------
- Bard is class which uses ranged weapon to make large distance attacks and obtain special items
  (musical notes). support skills are for temporal buffs  (mainly for allies like co-party members
  or pets/summons). Magic line is for solo attacks preferred on close distance.
- Dancer class similar with bard but have another solo skills coz not "sings" but "dancing". Main 
  feature of that skills are not to let be hitted. But still make damage.
- Musical notes are items to make combos or use in other skills as catalizator.
  (highest skill level don't use these items?)

description:
-------------

Редко вступающие в прямой конфликт, барды вплетают в свои песни магию,
что позволяет им избежать смерти, поддержать союзников и ослабить врагов.
--
Rarely in direct conflict, bards weave magic into their songs to avoid death,
support allies, and weaken enemies.

Танцовщицы - прекрасные и опасные недотроги. Чарующий танец, яд и клинок - вот в чём их сила.
--
Dancers are beautiful and dangerous touchy persons. The magic dance, the poison, and the blade -
 that's their power.

Музыканты - всеобщие любимцы, но не очень хорошие воины.
--
Musicians are everyone's favorites, but not very good warriors.
